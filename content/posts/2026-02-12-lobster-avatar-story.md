## 🎁 龙虾哥的 Prompt 盲盒

独乐乐不如众乐乐。我把这三版头像的 **Prompt（提示词）** 毫无保留地分享出来。
你可以把它们复制到 **ChatGPT (DALL-E 3)、豆包、通义千问、腾讯元宝** 里试试，效果都很顶！

**💡 使用技巧：** 把 "red lobster" 换成你喜欢的动物（比如 cat, dog, panda），就能生成你的专属头像！

### 1️⃣ 赛博朋克风 (Cyberpunk 3D)
`A cool anthropomorphic red lobster character, wearing futuristic programmer glasses, typing on a holographic keyboard, cyberpunk neon city background, 3D render, blender style, high quality, suitable for avatar.`

### 2️⃣ 极简 Logo 风 (Minimalist Vector)
`A flat vector logo icon of a red lobster head wearing thick black glasses, minimal design, orange and white, clean lines, suitable for tech startup logo, white background.`

### 3️⃣ 豆包奶萌风 (Cute Pixar Style)
`3D render of a cute cartoon red lobster character, wearing big round black glasses, typing on a laptop at a white desk, bright studio lighting, Pixar animation style, friendly and smart expression.`
